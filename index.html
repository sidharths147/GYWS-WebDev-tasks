About Us

Gopali Youth Welfare Society is a registered non-governmental voluntary organization primarily managed by some students and faculty members of IIT Kharagpur, with the support of local members from Gopali. It was established in 2002 with a mission to improve the living standard of the people of Gopali, a village 5 km from IIT Kharagpur. It was registered in 2004 under the West Bengal Societies registration act of 1961 with Reg. No S/IL/23893.
